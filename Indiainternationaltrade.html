Introduction

India's journey in international trade and globalisation has seen major transformations over decades. From restrictive policies during colonial rule to liberalisation in the 1990s and today's digital export economy, India has evolved to become a global player in trade and commerce.

Did You Know?

  • India was once known as the "Golden Bird" due to its prosperous trade and wealth.
  • The Silk Road connected India to China, Rome, and Central Asia for centuries.
  • India is the world's largest exporter of IT services today.
  • Chennai Port has been operating since 1881 and is one of the oldest in India.
  • The liberalisation policy of 1991 helped India avoid a financial crisis and opened up foreign investments.
